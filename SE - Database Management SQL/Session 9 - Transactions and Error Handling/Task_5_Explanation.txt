Galat trigger me kya problem thi:

Galat trigger me purchase amount ko balance me add kiya gaya tha.
Isliye payment karne ke baad balance kam hone ki jagah badh raha tha.

Example:
Balance 1000 tha.
Purchase amount 200 tha.
Galat result 1200 aa raha tha.

Maine kya fix kiya:

Plus sign ki jagah minus sign use kiya:

balance = balance - NEW.purchase_amount

Ab 1000 me se 200 spend karne par balance 800 aata hai.
