Difference Between Encapsulation and Abstraction


Encapsulation:

Data ko hide karke sirf getter aur setter methods se access karna.
Example: Instagram me phone number ya password private hota hai aur direct access nahi hota.

Abstraction:

User ko sirf important features dikhana aur internal working ko hide karna.
Example: WhatsApp me sirf Send button dikhai deta hai. Message background me kaise network se deliver hota hai, wo user ko nahi dikhaya jata.
